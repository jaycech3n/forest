Let {#A#} be a dcpo; then an element {#a:A#} is called *positively generated* when it is the supremum of the semidirected subset {#\brc{b:A\mid b=a\land \IsPos\,{a}}#}, i.e. we have {#a \sqsubseteq \bigsqcup_{p:\IsPos\,a}a#}.
