Evidently, the [kernel](jms-0009) of a morphism {#A\xrightarrow{f} B#} in a [category with zero morphisms](jms-000B) is the *equalizer* of  {#A\xrightarrow{f} B#} with the zero morphism {#A\xrightarrow{0} B#}.
