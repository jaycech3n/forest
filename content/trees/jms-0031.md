A [carrable map](jms-0033) {#\Mor{p}{E}{B}#} in a category {#\mathcal{C}#} is said to be *exponentiable* when it is [exponentiable](jms-0032) when viewed as an object of the slice category {#\Sl{\mathcal{C}}{B}#}.
