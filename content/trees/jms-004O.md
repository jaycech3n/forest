Let {#f : A \vdash B#} be a morphism in a deductive system {#\mathcal{D}#}. An *inverse* to {#f#} is defined to be a morphism {#g : B\vdash A#} such that {#f;g = \Idn{A}#} and {#g;f = \Idn{B}#}.
