Let {#A#} be a [pointed](jms-001S) dcpo; then the subposet {#A^+\subseteq A#} spanned by [positive](jms-001M) is a dcpo.

{{%proof%}}
This follows from the [closure of positive elements in an algebra under directed suprema](jms-001O).
{{%/proof%}}
