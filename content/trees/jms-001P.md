Let {#M#} be an {#\LiftMon#}-algebra; then subposet {#M^+\subseteq UM#} of {#M#} spanned by [positive](jms-001M) is a dcpo.

{{%proof%}}
This follows from the [closure of positive elements in an algebra under directed suprema](jms-001O).
{{%/proof%}}
