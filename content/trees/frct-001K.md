Let $E$ be displayed over $B$, and let $f:x\to y\in B$. Prove that a
morphism $\bar{f}:\bar{x}\to\Sub{f}\bar{y}$ is cartesian over $f$ in $E$ if and only
if $\bar{f}:\bar{y}\to\Sub{f}\bar{x}$ is cocartesian over $f$ in $\TotOpCat{E}$.
