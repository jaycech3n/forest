Let {#E#} be displayed over {#B#}, and let {#f:x\to y\in B#}. Prove that a
morphism {#\bar{f}:\bar{x}\DispTo{f}\bar{y}#} is cartesian over {#f#} in {#E#} if and only
if {#\bar{f}:\bar{y}\DispTo{f}\bar{x}#} is cocartesian over {#f#} in {#\TotOpCat{E}#}.
